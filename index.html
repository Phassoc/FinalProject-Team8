Machine Learning & Wine

Wine is a (delicious) alcoholic drink that dates back to 5000 BC. The red wine production process involves the extraction of color and flavor components from the grape skin. Red wine is made from dark-colored grape varieties.

Winemakers are investing in new technologies to improve the quality of wines. By assessing the most influential factors through physicochemical tests, we can use the outcomes to determine the relationship of the factors to the quality rating of the final wine produced.

After assembling the dataset, we used Python Pandas and Matplotlib to examine existing data on wine quality and then employed machine learning for regressions and predictions. Additionally, we include Tableau visualizations based on the existing data to show the factors our machine learning models are based on.
